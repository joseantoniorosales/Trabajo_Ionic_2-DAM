PENDING ENTERTAINMENT LIST

Tired of losing the track of the series/games/books you are into? You have had enough of forgeting those entertaining things that you said "Maybe later"? Worry not, because with Pending Entertainment List this won't happen to you anymore. You can choose between the lists of series, books and videogames (all of them only visible by you, of course), and add anything fitting the categories, be It something you are currently watching, reading or playing and keep track of your progress, or add those you want to start with in a future time.
